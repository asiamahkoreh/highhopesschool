admissions

Admissions are open all year round for all classes (Kindergaten to JHS 3).

Gaining admission to all academic levels is highly competitive, with procedures including completion of enrollment forms, interviews and assesment.

Parents / Guardians should visit the school to pick up an admission form and also for further inquiries. you can also contact the school for more information about enrollment. Admission is in progress.

For more info, contact us or send and email to aimhighhope@gmail.com

admission requirements

  1. You willl be examined after a successful interview.
  2. The examination will be in three (3) parts
    • English
    • Mathematics
    • General science
  3. Purchase the application form, complete and submit it to the admission office.
  4. Please attach to the completed forms your child's
    • Last two terminal reports / transcripts or testimonial from the last school.
    • Known examination results
    • Two (2) passport-size photographs
